FAQ

Common questions

How much does EV charger installation cost in Sydney?
Most standard home installs fall within a predictable fixed-price range; cable distance, switchboard condition and single vs three-phase supply are the main variables. Send photos and we'll give you an exact fixed price up front.
Do I need three-phase power?
No. A single-phase 7kW charger fully recharges most EVs overnight. If you have three-phase, we'll tell you honestly whether your car can actually use the extra speed before you pay for it.
Will my switchboard need an upgrade?
We assess this from your photos before quoting. If an upgrade is needed it's priced up front, and it improves the safety of your whole home, not just the charger circuit.
Can you install a charger I've already bought?
Yes, including Tesla Wall Connectors bought direct from Tesla, provided the unit is compliant for Australia. We confirm compatibility from the model number before booking.
