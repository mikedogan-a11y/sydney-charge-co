Questions

Answered before you ask

How much does EV charger installation cost in Sydney?
Most standard home installs fall within a predictable fixed-price range; cable distance, switchboard condition and single vs three-phase supply are the main variables. Send us photos of your switchboard and parking spot and we'll quote an exact fixed price before you commit to anything.
Do I need three-phase power to charge at home?
Rarely. A single-phase 7kW charger fully recharges most EVs overnight. If you have three-phase supply, we'll tell you honestly whether your car can actually use the extra speed before you pay for it.
Will my switchboard need an upgrade?
We assess this from your photos before quoting. If an upgrade is needed, it's priced up front, and it improves the safety of your whole home, not just the charger circuit.
Can you install a charger I've already bought?
Yes, including Tesla Wall Connectors bought direct from Tesla, provided the unit is compliant for use in Australia. We confirm compatibility from the model number before booking.
