Straight answers

The stuff everyone asks

What does an EV charger install cost?
Most standard home installs land in a predictable fixed-price range. Cable distance, switchboard condition and single vs three-phase are what move it. Send photos, get an exact number. No call-out fee, no obligation.
Do I need three-phase power?
Nah, usually not. A single-phase 7kW charger fills most EVs overnight. Got three-phase? We'll tell you straight whether your car can actually use the extra speed before you pay for it.
Will my switchboard cope?
We check from your photos before quoting. If it needs an upgrade, it's priced up front and makes your whole house safer, not just the charger circuit.
Bought your own charger already?
All good. We install BYO units including Tesla Wall Connectors bought direct, as long as they're compliant for Australia. We confirm from the model number before booking.
