Questions

Answered before you ask

What does installation cost?
Most residential installations fall within a predictable fixed-price range; cable distance, switchboard condition and supply type are the main variables. We quote an exact figure from photographs before any commitment is made.
Is three-phase power necessary?
Rarely. A single-phase 7kW charger replenishes most vehicles overnight. Where three-phase is available, we advise honestly on whether your vehicle can make use of it before you spend the difference.
Will the switchboard require attention?
We assess this from your photographs before quoting. Where an upgrade is warranted, it is priced up front and improves the safety of the whole home, not merely the charging circuit.
May we supply our own charger?
Certainly, including Tesla Wall Connectors purchased directly, provided the unit is compliant for use in Australia. We confirm compatibility from the model number before booking.
